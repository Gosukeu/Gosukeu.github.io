Pokémon FireRed: Rocket Edition

Get The Rom

Description

Unlike traditional Pokémon games where you're a heroic trainer, in Rocket Edition, you play as a Team Rocket grunt working under Giovanni. Your goal is to steal Pokémon, rise through the ranks, and uncover the secrets behind the corruption in Kanto.

Key Features:

  • You can steal Pokémon from trainers, depending on their status (e.g., stealing from rich NPCs is easier).
  • You can choose to be a loyal Rocket or betray the organization.
  • A darker twist to the classic FireRed story.
  • New areas, events, and secrets that weren’t in the original FireRed.
