Description

Pokémon Crystal is an enhanced version of Pokémon Gold & Silver, released in 2000 (Japan) and 2001 (Worldwide) for the Game Boy Color (GBC). It is the first Pokémon game with animated sprites and introduces new story elements, making it the definitive Gen 2 experience.

Key Features:

  • Defeat all 8 Gym Leaders and become the Johto Champion.
  • Stop Team Rocket, who is trying to revive their leader Giovanni.
  • Catch the Legendary Beast Suicune, which has a bigger role in Crystal.
  • Travel to Kanto after the Elite Four and battle Red, the strongest trainer.
