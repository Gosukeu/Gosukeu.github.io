Description

Pokémon HeartGold is a remake of Pokémon Gold, originally released for the Game Boy Color in 1999. It was released in 2009 in Japan and 2010 worldwide for the Nintendo DS. Along with its counterpart, Pokémon SoulSilver (which is a remake of Pokémon Silver), it returns players to the Johto Region with updated graphics, mechanics, and new features.

Key Features:

  • Team Rocket: The evil team from Pokémon Gold/Silver returns with plans to steal Pokémon and cause trouble.
  • Legendary Pokémon: Players can encounter and capture Ho-Oh (the Legendary of HeartGold) and Lugia (the Legendary of SoulSilver), along with other Legendary creatures like Raikou, Entei, Suicune, and the three Legendary Birds.
  • Becoming the Champion: Players aim to defeat the Gym Leaders, the Elite Four, and the Champion at the Indigo Plateau.
  • Choose from Chikorita, Cyndaquil, or Totodile as your starter Pokémon.
