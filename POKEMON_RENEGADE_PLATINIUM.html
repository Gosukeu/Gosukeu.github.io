Pokémon Renegade Platinum

Description

Pokémon Renegade Platinum is an enhanced difficulty and quality-of-life ROM hack of Pokémon Platinum. Created by Drayano, a well-known ROM hacker, this game rebalances battles, adds all Pokémon, and improves trainer teams while keeping the Sinnoh experience intact.

Key Features:

  • All 493 Pokémon are available (catch them without trading).
  • Higher difficulty – Gym Leaders, Elite Four, and trainers have stronger teams.
  • Reworked difficulty, making battles more strategic.
  • Improved trainer AI for more challenging battles.
