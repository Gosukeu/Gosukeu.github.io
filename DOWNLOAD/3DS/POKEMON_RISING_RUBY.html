Pokémon Rising Ruby

Description

Pokémon Rising Ruby & Sinking Sapphire are ROM hacks of Pokémon Omega Ruby & Alpha Sapphire designed to provide a more challenging and rewarding experience for veteran players. These hacks introduce various difficulty improvements, rebalanced Pokémon, and changes to the gameplay, making it more exciting and strategic. They also allow for all Pokémon from Generations 1-7 to be caught within the game, eliminating the need for external transfers or trading.

Key Features:

  • Increased Difficulty – Trainers, Gym Leaders, and the Elite Four are much stronger, with updated team compositions and higher-level Pokémon.
  • Improved AI – Opponents have smarter strategies, making battles feel like real competitive encounters.
  • Mega Evolution Changes – Some Pokémon have received updated or new Mega Evolutions, adding a fresh twist to battles.
