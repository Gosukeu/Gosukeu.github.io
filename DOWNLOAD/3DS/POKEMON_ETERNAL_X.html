Pokémon Eternal X

Get The Rom

Description

Pokémon Eternal X & Wilting Y are ROM hacks of Pokémon X and Y, designed to provide a more challenging, balanced, and rewarding experience while keeping the core story and world intact. This hack enhances trainer battles, makes all Pokémon available, and improves moves, abilities, and evolution methods.

Key Features:

  • All 721 Pokémon Available – Every Pokémon from Generations 1-6 can be caught or obtained in the game.
  • Three Game Modes: Legal Version – Keeps changes minimal while ensuring all Pokémon are available. Rebalanced Version – Adjusts weaker Pokémon and moves for a fairer experience. Insanity Version – The hardest mode with maximized difficulty and more aggressive trainers.
  • Expanded Move & Ability Sets – Many Pokémon have updated movesets, abilities, and stat changes for better balance.
