Description

Pokémon Sun is the first installment in Generation 7, introducing a brand-new region, Alola, and breaking the traditional Pokémon formula by removing Gyms in favor of Island Trials. The game features Z-Moves, new regional Pokémon called Alolan Forms, and a more immersive story compared to previous games.

Key Features:

  • Island Trials & Totem Pokémon – Instead of Gyms, trainers complete trials and battle powered-up Totem Pokémon.
  • Z-Moves – A new mechanic where Pokémon unleash one super-powerful move per battle.
  • Alolan Forms – Classic Pokémon like Alolan Raichu, Alolan Vulpix, and Alolan Marowak have new designs and types.
