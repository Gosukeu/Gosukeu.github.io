Description

Pokémon Radical Red is a FireRed-based ROM hack that offers a challenging and competitive Pokémon experience, designed for hardcore players and those looking for a more engaging battle system. It features Pokémon up to Gen 8, smarter AI, and a built-in Randomizer & Nuzlocke mode. This hack removes grinding by boosting experience gain, allowing you to focus purely on strategic battles. If you’re looking for the ultimate difficulty and modern mechanics in a classic GBA game, Radical Red is the perfect choice!

Key Features:

  • Smarter trainers that use competitive strategies
  • Includes Gen 6-8 battle mechanics
  • Including hidden abilities
  • No easy battles here!
