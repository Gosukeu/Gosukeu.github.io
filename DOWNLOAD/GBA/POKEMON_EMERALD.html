Description

Pokémon Emerald is the enhanced version of Pokémon Ruby and Sapphire, released for the Game Boy Advance (GBA) in 2004 (Japan) and 2005 (Worldwide). It is considered one of the best classic Pokémon games due to its expanded story, battle improvements, and post-game content.

Key Features:

  • Defeat all 8 Gym Leaders and become the Hoenn Champion.
  • Stop Team Magma & Team Aqua, who are trying to awaken Groudon and Kyogre.
  • Unlike Ruby & Sapphire, both villainous teams (Magma & Aqua) are active in Emerald, making the story more dynamic.
  • Catch Rayquaza, the legendary Pokémon that can stop the catastrophe caused by Groudon & Kyogre.
