Description

Pokémon Unbound is one of the most advanced and feature-packed Pokémon ROM hacks ever created, built on the Pokémon FireRed engine. Set in the Borrius Region, a land once ravaged by war, you take on the role of a young trainer caught in the midst of a new conflict involving the Shadow Pokémon Cult. With a gripping story-driven adventure, modern mechanics, and deep customization, Pokémon Unbound delivers a fresh and challenging Pokémon experience unlike any other.

Key Features:

  • Mega Evolution, Dynamax, & Z-Moves
  • Includes Pokémon from Gen 1 to Gen 8
  • Expansive post-game content
  • Competitive AI
