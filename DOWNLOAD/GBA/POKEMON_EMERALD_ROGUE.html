Pokémon Emerald Rogue

Get The Rom

Description

Pokémon Emerald Rogue is a unique roguelike twist on Pokémon Emerald, where instead of a traditional storyline, every adventure is randomized and different. You’ll progress through a series of randomized routes, battling trainers, catching Pokémon, and gathering items until you reach the Champion. If you lose, you start over from the beginning with only a few carryover perks, making each run challenging and exciting!

Key Features:

  • Every journey is randomized, creating a new experience each time
  • Each route has different biomes, wild Pokémon, and trainers
  • Includes Mega Evolution, Fairy-type, and more
  • Play casually or try hardcore Nuzlocke Mode
