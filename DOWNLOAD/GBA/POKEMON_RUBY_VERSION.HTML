Description

Pokémon Ruby is a Gen 3 Pokémon game released in 2002 (Japan) and 2003 (Worldwide) for the Game Boy Advance (GBA). It introduced the Hoenn region, Double Battles, and Abilities, marking a major upgrade from previous games.Ruby is paired with Pokémon Sapphire, but they have differences, such as exclusive Pokémon and different villain teams (Team Magma vs. Team Aqua).

Key Features:

  • Defeat 8 Gym Leaders and challenge the Elite Four to become the Hoenn Champion.
  • Stop Team Magma, a villainous group trying to awaken Groudon to expand the land.
  • Catch and train over 200 Pokémon, including brand-new Hoenn Pokémon.
  • Battle your rival (May/Brendan) and other trainers like Wally, who grows stronger over time.
