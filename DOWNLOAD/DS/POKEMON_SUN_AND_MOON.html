Pokémon Diamond Sun and Moon

Get The Rom

Description

Pokémon Diamond Sun and Moon is a ROM hack of the original Pokémon Diamond game for the Nintendo DS, created by ROM hackers Grillo & Lurge. This fan-made modification integrates elements from the seventh-generation Pokémon Sun and Moon games into the Diamond version, offering a unique gameplay experience.

Key Features:

  • Seventh-Generation Pokémon: The game introduces Pokémon from the Alola region, allowing players to catch and train species that were not available in the original Diamond game.
  • Updated Playable Characters: Players can choose to play as characters from the seventh generation, providing a fresh perspective compared to the original protagonists.
  • Evolution Adjustments: Pokémon that originally required trading to evolve have been modified to evolve through other methods, facilitating a more complete single-player experience.
  • Trainer and Sprite Updates: Many in-game trainers have updated teams, and some Pokémon sprites have been refreshed to align with newer designs.
