Description

Pokémon Diamond was released in 2006 in Japan and 2007 worldwide. It marks the beginning of Generation IV and takes place in the Sinnoh Region. Alongside its counterpart, Pokémon Pearl, it introduced many new gameplay features, such as online trading and battling, new battle mechanics, and a fresh storyline with unique Pokémon and legendary creatures.

Key Features:

  • The quest to stop Team Galactic, who are trying to capture Dialga to distort time for their own purposes.
  • Catching Legendary Pokémon like Dialga, Uxie, Mesprit, and Azelf.
  • Becoming the Champion after defeating the Gym Leaders and Elite Four.
  • Introduces 107 new Pokémon, including Turtwig, Chimchar, and Piplup as the starter Pokémon.
