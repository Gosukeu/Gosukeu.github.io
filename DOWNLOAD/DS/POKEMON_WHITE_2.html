Description

Pokémon White 2 is the official sequel to Pokémon White, released for the Nintendo DS in 2012. It takes place in the Unova region, two years after the events of the original Pokémon Black & White, introducing new characters, locations, and gameplay mechanics.

Key Features:

  • Expanded Unova Region: New areas, including Virbank City, Humilau City, and the Black Tower/White Treehollow. Some routes and towns have changed over time.
  • New Pokémon & National Dex Available Early: Unlike Pokémon Black & White, Pokémon from previous generations are available right from the start.
  • Battle Gym Leaders & Champions from previous generations, including Red, Blue, Cynthia, and Lance. One of the best post-game features in Pokémon history.
  • Choose from Snivy, Tepig, or Oshawott as your starter Pokémon.
