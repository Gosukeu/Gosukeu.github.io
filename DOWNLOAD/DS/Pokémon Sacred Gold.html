Description

Pokémon Sacred Gold & Storm Silver are enhanced difficulty ROM hacks of HeartGold & SoulSilver. These hacks improve the original experience by making the game more challenging, increasing Pokémon availability, and adding quality-of-life features.

Key Features:

  • Every Pokémon from Gen 1–4 is available (including event Pokémon like Mew, Celebi, and Arceus).
  • Increased difficulty for trainers, Gym Leaders, and Elite Four.
  • New moves, abilities, and updated stats for balance.
  • Fairy-type added, along with buffs to certain Pokémon.
