Description

Pokémon White 2 Redux is an enhanced difficulty and quality-of-life ROM hack of Pokémon White 2. It improves balance, makes all Pokémon available, and enhances battles while keeping the core Unova experience intact.

Key Features:

  • All 649 Pokémon are obtainable without trading.
  • Gym Leaders & Elite Four have better, more challenging teams.
  • More challenging AI (smarter opponents, better strategies).
  • Improved Pokémon movesets, abilities, and stats.
