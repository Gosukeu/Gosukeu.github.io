Description

Pokémon Platinum is an enhanced version of Pokémon Diamond & Pearl, released for the Nintendo DS in 2008 (Japan) and 2009 (worldwide). It introduces new story elements, improved gameplay, and additional features that make it the definitive Generation 4 Pokémon game.

Key Features:

  • Team Galactic's Expanded Role = The villainous group has a more prominent storyline, including a showdown in the newly introduced Distortion World.
  • The Distortion World = A mysterious, physics-defying realm where the Legendary Pokémon Giratina resides.
  • New Post-Game Content = Additional areas like the Battle Frontier, new Legendary Pokémon, and enhanced gameplay features.
  • Elite Four & Gym Leader Rematches = Stronger versions of Gym Leaders and the Elite Four appear after the main story.
