Pokémon Silver Yellow

Get The Rom

Description

Pokémon Silver Yellow is a ROM hack based on Pokémon Yellow but with elements from Pokémon Silver. It's an interesting take that combines the classic feel of Pokémon Yellow with aspects of the Johto region from Pokémon Silver. Here's a quick breakdown of what to expect:

Key Features:

  • Pikachu as the starter: Just like in Pokémon Yellow, Pikachu is your partner.
  • Johto Pokémon added: The hack incorporates Johto Pokémon, making it a hybrid between Kanto and Johto regions.
  • New rival encounters: More rival battles featuring some of the Johto leaders and characters from Pokémon Silver.
  • Bug fixes: Fixes some of the old game’s glitches and other issues for a smoother experience.
