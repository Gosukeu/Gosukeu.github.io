Pokémon Moon Black 2

Get The Rom

Description

Pokémon Moon Black 2 is a fan-made ROM hack of Pokémon Black 2 for the Nintendo DS, developed by JrFort and Aster. This modification integrates elements from the seventh generation of Pokémon games, particularly Pokémon Sun and Moon, into the Unova region's adventure.

Key Features:

  • New Pokémon: Inclusion of species from the Unova, Kalos, Alola, and Galar regions.
  • Mega Evolution: Available outside of battle, allowing certain Pokémon to evolve into more powerful forms.
  • Regional Forms: Introduction of Unova and Alola forms, offering new typings and designs for familiar Pokémon.
  • Updated Characters: New rivals, Gym Leaders, Elite Four members, and a new Champion to challenge.
