Description

Pokémon Supernova Sun & Penumbra Moon are ROM hacks of Pokémon Ultra Sun and Ultra Moon, designed to increase difficulty, rebalance gameplay, and make all Pokémon obtainable without external transfers. These hacks are perfect for players who want a tougher, more strategic Pokémon experience while still enjoying the Alola region.

Key Features:

  • Harder Trainer Battles – Trainers, Gym Leaders, and the Elite Four have stronger teams with better movesets.
  • Smarter AI – Opponents now make better decisions, making battles feel more like real competitive matches.
  • All Pokémon Catchable – Every Pokémon from Generations 1-7 can be found in the game, eliminating the need for trading. from past games.
