Description

Pokémon Black is one of the two versions of Generation V, released in 2010 for the Nintendo DS, alongside Pokémon White. It takes place in the Unova region, which is inspired by New York City, and introduces a brand-new set of Pokémon, new battle mechanics, and a deeper story than previous generations.

Key Features:

  • Defeat 8 Gym Leaders and challenge the Elite Four to become the Unova Champion.
  • Stop Team Plasma, a villainous team that aims to liberate Pokémon from trainers.
  • Uncover the mystery of Reshiram, the Legendary Dragon Pokémon, and discover the truth about the relationship between humans and Pokémon.
  • Catch and train over 150 new Pokémon, including legendary Pokémon like Reshiram and Zekrom.
